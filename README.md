# TVS Eurogrip TORQ Challenge 3.0
### National Finalist | Top 28 out of 41,500+ Teams (Top 0.07%)

> A strategic consulting project focused on strengthening TVS Eurogrip's position in India's two-wheeler tyre market through market research, competitive analysis, customer insights, and a scalable go-to-market strategy.

---

## Project Overview

TVS Eurogrip, despite having a strong manufacturing base, global presence, and advanced R&D capabilities, faces challenges in customer trust, brand recall, and aftermarket penetration.

This project aimed to identify the root causes behind these challenges and recommend a comprehensive business growth strategy to improve market share, customer loyalty, and long-term brand positioning.

---

## Business Objective

Develop a strategic roadmap that enables TVS Eurogrip to:

- Increase brand awareness among Gen-Z and young riders.
- Improve trust among mechanics and end customers.
- Strengthen dealer engagement and aftermarket sales.
- Drive customer retention through loyalty initiatives.
- Build a scalable regional expansion strategy.

---

## Key Insights

### Customer Pain Points
- Low brand recall among younger riders.
- Legacy association with "TVS Tyres" affecting premium positioning.
- Customer concerns regarding quality and durability.
- Limited post-purchase engagement.

### Market Opportunity
- Indian Two-Wheeler Tyre Market: ~USD 3 Billion
- Estimated Market CAGR: 10–12%
- Presence in over 85 countries
- Strong manufacturing and R&D capabilities
