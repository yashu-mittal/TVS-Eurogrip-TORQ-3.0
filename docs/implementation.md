## Executable Ideas

### Traditional Marketing — Loyalty Program
- **Localized Loyalty Points:** Tehsil-level incentives; local engagement (localized events, test-ride sessions, demo days).
- **Integrated Digital & Offline Rewards:** Dealer check-ins, referral bonuses.
- **Tiered Benefits:** Exclusive access for top-tier members.

### Mechanic Advocacy Program
- **Training & Certification:** Dealer-led workshops; certified mechanic program.
- **Reward Integration:** Point accumulation (redeemable for tools, exclusive merchandise, financial incentives); transparency & trust via dealer dashboard.
- **Dealer-Managed Tracking System:** Digital referral codes; app-based dashboard for real-time tracking of mechanic-referred sales.

### Distributor/Hub Owner Partnership Program
- **Tiered Distributor Incentives:** Silver, Gold, Platinum tiers based on regional sales targets.
- **Joint Marketing & Training Initiatives:** Distributor training, co-branded marketing collateral, regional events.
- **Feedback & Continuous Improvement:** Performance dashboards and regular reviews.

### Increasing Digital Reach
- **Channels:** Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, X (Twitter), Snapchat — chosen to increase reach, relevance, and resonance.
- **Instagram Influencers:** Mintu & Rahul, King Indian, Biker Diaries, Two Wheeler Talk.
- **Micro-Influencers (Tier 1/2 cities, 20K–100K followers):** MotoVlogger India (@motovloggerindia), The Indian Biker (@theindianbiker), OffRoad India (@offroadindia), SheRidesIndia (@sheridesindia), Madhya Pradesh Rider (@mprider).

### Financial Benefits Expected
- Sales growth expected at **20–25% YoY** in the premium segment.
- Additional incremental revenue estimated at **₹120–180 crore** in the first year post-campaign.
- Tyre market growing at a CAGR of 7.6%, with premium segments expanding faster — positions Eurogrip to capture a higher share of the premium/value-added segment.
- Enhanced brand visibility expected to generate more public investment in the company.
- **20–40% higher marketing ROI**, with cost savings through optimization.
